# Project Requirements: ARK Interior Design & Architecture Project Management System

## 1. Project Overview
A comprehensive web-based project management system tailored for interior design and architecture firms. It covers the entire project lifecycle from lead generation to final handover, integrating financial tracking, team management, and real-time site monitoring.

## 2. Core Modules
### 2.1 User Management
- **Roles**: Super Admin, Admin/Principal Architect, Accountant, Project Head, Site Engineer.
- **Features**: Secure login, role-based access control, profile management.

### 2.2 Client & Lead Management
- **Leads**: Track enquiries from various sources, maintain follow-ups, and monitor conversion rates.
- **Clients**: Automated conversion from lead to client, centralized client records, and proposal generation.

### 2.3 Project Management
- **Lifecycle**: Concept → Design → Execution → Handover.
- **Dashboards**: Project-specific views showing progress, budget status (₹), and team info.
- **Timeline**: Visual Gantt chart for detailed scheduling (site prep, electrical, plumbing, civil, carpentry, painting, finishing).

### 2.4 Team & Workload Management
- **Staff Allocation**: Assign Project Heads and Site Engineers to specific projects.
- **Workload Balance**: Visual tracking to ensure no team member is overloaded.
- **Attendance**: Site-wise attendance tracking using mobile-friendly interfaces.

### 2.5 Finance & Inventory
- **Inventory**: Project-specific inventory tracking to prevent material leakage.
- **Budgeting**: Category-wise budget allocation and real-time "Spent vs. Remaining" tracking.
- **Expenses**: Tracking material, labor, transportation, and site costs.
- **GST Invoicing**: Professional GST invoice generation and client payment logs.

### 2.6 Analytics
- Role-specific dashboards with real-time KPIs (Total Revenue, Active Projects, Delayed Tasks, etc.).

- **Frontend**: React.js
- **Backend**: PHP (RESTful APIs)
- **Database**: MySQL
- **Environment**: Local deployment via XAMPP

## 5. Key Success Metrics
- 100% visibility into project-wise inventory and expenses.
- Real-time tracking of project timelines to minimize delays.
- Standardized financial reporting for all projects.
